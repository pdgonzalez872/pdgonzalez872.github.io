On platform tennis
Paulo Gonzalez
platform tennis, racket sports, health

I wanted to write about platform tennis. It's a great racket sport I picked up after college that helped me in many ways in my life. It helped me develop my body (my shoulder doubled in size), allowed me to improve my volleys and work on my favorite part of the game: overheads. I had a blast competing, playing the Chicago league and traveling to tournaments.

I eventually made a decision to not return to platform after the 2020 break. It had become less of a priority and injury was just a matter of time.
