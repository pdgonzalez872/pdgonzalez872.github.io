On platform tennis
Paulo Gonzalez
sql, postgres, dbeaver

I wanted to write about platform tennis. It's a great racket sport I picked up after college. It was the sport that helped me develop my body (my shoulder doubled in size) and I had a blast playing it, the league, the travel tour. The world stopped in March 2020 and I eventually made a decision to not return to platform. stop playing because while it started to not become a priority for me

As far as playing level, I played well, but didn't play it the right way. I did it my way, a fun way to spent my time doing my hobby. Platform can get very slow and I never adapted to what was needed to really win a platform match. I didn't want to play 12 minute points like my good friend Randy Lofgren, nor play a 6hr match like Graham and Adam did. While I'm super proud of their achievements, that's just not for me and that's ok. I've learned to deal with it. Add youtube video
